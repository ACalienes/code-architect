What this is

Like any growing organization, the "who works here?" list lives in three different places for different purposes. (1) The static manifests under knowledge/manifests/ are the spec / job descriptions — what each agent IS and what it CAN do. (2) ~/.kameha/agents.json is the HR roster — who's currently employed, what's their inbox, are they active. (3) mesh.db.agents is the phone directory — who has a working mesh route, who can send to whom, what's the tier.

Why it matters to you

When all three agree (top 6 rows), the agent works normally. When they disagree, weird things happen: agents that work in practice but aren't in the spec (chronicle, conductor — no job description), agents whose spec says they exist but aren't on the HR roster (framer, acd — no employee badge), and one outright ghost (kmg — real code, zero paperwork). Fixing this is most of the "owners.json bootstrap" → "mesh route seeding" work in W4+.
