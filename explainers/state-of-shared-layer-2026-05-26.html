03 What five review rounds hardened

Each round was adversarial; each finding was folded and re-proven. The core (isolation, auth, transactions, schema) kept coming back verified-closed — the findings narrowed to one sub-system, then stopped.

Round 1–2
Bypass & contracts

Sealed the facade; signed the legacy bridge; schema-gated promotion; admin-only / insert-only enrollment; transactions on every multi-step write.

Round 3
Atomicity & crypto

Atomic promote; canonicalization rejects non-JSON; health catches a wedged client; enrollment can't hand out mismatched keys.

Round 4
The symlink critical

Single-writer read-only projection (no client dir-write → no symlink swap); reject non-plain objects; central-DB permission gate.

Round 5
Ack accounting

No schema bypass on ingress; no ack-store starvation or false-wedge; projection perms match the runbook; client drainer requires its ack-store.

+ proactive
